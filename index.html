Core Technology

The cryptographic engines powering the Nova Network.

Zero-Knowledge Proof

We verify *that* you can vote, without knowing *who* you are. Your identity is cryptographically detached from your ballot.

Immutable Chain

Every vote is salt-hashed and linked to the previous one. Modifying a past record breaks the entire chain, ensuring 100% integrity.

Decentralized Storage

Data is sharded and stored across multiple nodes (simulated via Firebase Clusters), preventing a single point of failure.
